Top three activities to do in Bydgoszcz

Attend a concert

Pomeranian philharmonic is a great place to spend your night out.

Picnic at Myślęcinek Park

The largest town park in Poland is perfect for picnic, family fun, outdoor sports or just chilling out.

Get sweaty at the sport fields

Play volleyball, football, basketball, lift some weights or rent a kayak boat.
